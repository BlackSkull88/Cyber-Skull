About

I'm a student, a hacker, a coder, and a web designer. It all started when I made my first game with the help of youtube videos, but It started to get frustrating for me, and as I was searching on "How to be a programmer" I saw a video about hacking passwords.

I fell in love with the idea of hacking. I booted my virtual machines, I knew a lot of hacks. but... I got bored. it was not entertaining or exciting anymore, I found new hobbies like skating.

One day I look at my computer, I boot it up again, and it hit me! I just needed a break. Hours spent on the computer, my brain needed a break, a long one. But now I'm back with most of the things forgotten, still learning, but now it's more exciting.

I'm back, stronger than ever. "The quit" is just a break.

--Cyber-Skull
